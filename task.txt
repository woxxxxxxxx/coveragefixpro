# CoverageFixPro UI修复 + SEO + Logo

## Step 1：检查首页SEO
查看 C:\Users\Administrator\coveragefixpro\index.html head部分
确认是否有 og:title / og:description / canonical / JSON-LD
缺什么补什么：

<meta name="description" content="120+ free insurance calculators for auto, health, life, home and business insurance. Estimate premiums, coverage needs and savings instantly. No signup required.">
<link rel="canonical" href="https://coveragefixpro.com/">
<meta property="og:title" content="Insurance Calculators & Tools - Free Online | CoverageFixPro">
<meta property="og:description" content="120+ free insurance calculators. Estimate auto, health, life, home and business insurance premiums instantly.">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://coveragefixpro.com/">
<meta property="og:type" content="website">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://coveragefixpro.com/og-image.png">
<script type="application/ld+json">
{"@context":"https://schema.org","@type":"WebSite","name":"CoverageFixPro","url":"https://coveragefixpro.com","description":"Free insurance calculators for auto, health, life, home and business insurance"}
</script>
