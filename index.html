Frequently Asked Questions

Are these insurance calculators free? ▼
Yes, all 120+ calculators on CoverageFixPro are completely free to use. No account, subscription, or credit card is required. Simply enter your information and get instant estimates.
How accurate are the premium estimates? ▼
Our calculators provide ballpark estimates based on industry averages and published rate factors. Actual premiums vary by insurer, your exact credit history, ZIP code, and other factors. Use these results as a starting point, then get real quotes from licensed agents.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No account is needed. All calculations happen instantly in your browser. We do not store any personal information you enter into our tools.
What is a deductible in insurance? ▼
A deductible is the amount you pay out-of-pocket before your insurance kicks in. For example, with a $500 deductible, you pay the first $500 of a claim and insurance covers the rest (up to policy limits).
How much auto insurance do I need? ▼
Most states require minimum liability coverage. However, financial advisors recommend carrying enough liability to protect your assets — typically at least $100,000/$300,000 for bodily injury. Use our Auto Liability Coverage Calculator for a personalized recommendation.
What does health insurance cover? ▼
ACA-compliant plans must cover 10 essential health benefits including emergency care, hospitalization, maternity, mental health, prescription drugs, and preventive services. Coverage specifics vary by plan type (HMO, PPO, HDHP) and insurer.
How much life insurance do I need? ▼
A common rule of thumb is 10-12 times your annual income. However, your actual need depends on debts, dependents, income replacement needs, and existing savings. Our life insurance calculators help you determine the right amount.
What does homeowners insurance cover? ▼
Standard homeowners insurance (HO-3) covers the dwelling structure, personal property, liability protection, and additional living expenses if your home is uninhabitable. It typically excludes floods and earthquakes, which require separate policies.
Can I use these tools on mobile? ▼
Yes, all CoverageFixPro calculators are fully responsive and mobile-friendly. They work on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers without any app download required.
How often are the calculators updated? ▼
We review and update our calculators regularly to reflect current federal guidelines (such as ACA limits and Medicare premiums), IRS contribution limits, and industry rate trends. Figures are current as of 2026.
